About this site

This is a student‑run hub for UC Merced events. It’s not an official university site. Our goal is to make it simple to see what’s going on around campus at a glance.

  • Mobile‑first, fast, and accessible.
  • Map view shows every event we’ve received.
  • Open to all student orgs, departments, and teams.

Submit an event

Have something to share? Send us the details and we’ll add it quickly.

  1. Event title, date/time, and a short description.
  2. Host/organization and location (building/room or outdoor spot).
  3. Optional: a link for RSVPs or more info.
